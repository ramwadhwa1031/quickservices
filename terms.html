Service Infrastructure Logic (T&C)

1. Operational Diagnosis Agreement

Upon initiating a diagnostic command request on our Pune array, the property owner automatically authorizes our technicians to open and analyze the defective hardware architecture.

2. Hardware Safety Clause

QuickCare is strictly not liable for pre-existing gas leaks in built-in hobs caused by inadequate core pipeline connections. We solely operate on the designated appliance hardware logic block.

3. Service Footprint Charge

If a technician completes a flawless diagnosis and details the exact part requirement, but the user rejects the final install block, a default minimum operative logic fee stands mandatory for the dispatch.

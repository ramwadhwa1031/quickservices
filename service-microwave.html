Comprehensive Microwave Ovens Diagnostics

Microwave ovens utilize extremely high-voltage transformers and magnetrons that can decay over time. If your oven stops heating, sparks, or the touch panel freezes, it requires immediate certified attention. We safely discharge the capacitors, test the diode logic, and replace the magnetron with OEM parts to restore absolute microwave radiation distribution.
